# Bakery Website

Welcome to the official repository for our Bakery Website! This platform allows customers to browse our delicious menu, place orders online, and discover everything our bakery has to offer.

## 🥐 Project Overview

Our website showcases a variety of bakery items and provides a seamless experience for online ordering, customer interaction, and more.

## ✨ Features

- **Browse Menu**: Explore our cakes, breads, pastries, and more.
- **Online Ordering**: Place orders and pay securely online.
- **User Accounts**: Register and log in for a personalized experience.
- **Responsive Design**: Works beautifully on mobile and desktop devices.
- **Customer Reviews**: Read and submit testimonials.
- **Contact & Location**: Find us on the map or send us a message.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Frontend: (e.g., React, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap)
- Database: (my sql)
- Payment Integration: (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
- Deployment: (e.g., Vercel, Netlify, Heroku)
